About Me

I am a Memphis-born, Cleveland-raised aspiring full stack developer. Growing up in Cleveland Heights, I attended school through the CHUH school system. I am the youngest of 5, which includes my two half brothers. I've always gravitated to technology since I was a child, the biggest medium being video games. Naturally, like any respectable gamer, I've dreamed of making my own game for a long time now. This is a passion project that I hope to complete, but coding has opened my eyes to the world of possibilities available to me.

I'm fond of camping, traveling, and cinema as well. This year I've already been on 5 trips, the latest in New Orleans! Working hard means you need to play hard too, right? I just happened to front-load my fun before the work. Even so, I hope to take at least one more trip, to see my sisters in California.

Within this course, I'm hoping I will get a foundation to build upon. Coding isnt something anyone can expect to perfect in 6 months, and i expect this to be the first of many steps taken to that end. I would be happy with what I receive from this class if I'm able to get a job and support my family using the skills that I've developed. As I earlier stated, a dream would be to be able to program and release a game of my own, even if only on a small-scale app store.
